How to Play

Your goal is to match each image with its duplicate. Select your difficulty level to memorize the images: 3, 5, or 8 seconds. Once the timer ends, images will be hidden, and you'll have limited time to solve the puzzle:

  • 8 photos - 120 seconds
  • 10 photos - 150 seconds
  • 12 photos - 180 seconds
